Jan. 2025 – Present · Munich, Germany

My work emphasizes offloading computationally demanding modules to edge and cloud servers, enhancing onboard processing efficiency and expanding payload capacity. I’ve also contributed to the design of AI-powered navigation and planning tools that operate over continuously updated 3D world models built from multi-modal sensor data.
