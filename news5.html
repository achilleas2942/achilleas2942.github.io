🇸🇪 Reglermötet 2022 — Swedish Control Conference 8–9 Jun 2022

Attended Reglermötet 2022, the annual Swedish Control Conference.

Read more

On June 8–9, 2022, I attended Reglermötet 2022, the annual Swedish Control Conference (Reglermötet). The conference brings together the Swedish automatic control community from academia and industry to share research results, network, and discuss the future of control systems in Sweden. As an early-stage Ph.D. researcher, participating in this national event provided valuable exposure to the Swedish research landscape.

⛏️ Aitik Mine Visit — G-DRONES Field Campaign 7 Jun 2022

First field visit to the Aitik copper mine as part of the G-DRONES project.

Read more

On June 7th 2022, I visited the Aitik open-pit copper mine in Gällivare, Sweden, for the first time as part of the G-DRONES Vinnova project. This initial site visit allowed us to assess the environment and requirements for deploying autonomous drone systems in one of Europe's largest open-pit mines, laying the groundwork for future experimental campaigns.

⛏️ 5GEIM Project Kickoff Experiments, Skellefteå 31 Mar – 1 Apr 2022

⛏️ 5GEIM Project Kickoff 2021

Read more

The 5G Edge Innovations for Mining (5GEIM) Vinnova project officially kicked off in 2021. 🤖 MBZIRC 2023 — Mohamed Bin Zayed International Robotics Challenge 2023

Contributed to Team ROC for the MBZIRC 2023 international robotics competition.

Read more

In 2023, I contributed to Team ROC for the Mohamed Bin Zayed International Robotics Challenge (MBZIRC), one of the world's most prestigious robotics competitions. The MBZIRC challenges teams to solve complex real-world robotics problems in dynamic environments, with a focus on autonomous aerial and ground systems.
